Core Features

Built for battles

Every feature designed around the fight. Roll your fate, master your species, and dominate the leaderboards.

🎲
Species Rolling

Roll for one of 20+ species ranging from common Half-Blood (26%) to the legendary Demi God (0.5%). Each roll is permanent until you switch.

⚔️
Turn-Based Combat

Full RPG fights with attack, heal, and ULT actions. Every species has a unique passive and ultimate ability that changes how you play.

🤖
Bot Fights

Challenge AI opponents across 4 difficulties: Baby Bot, Warrior Bot, Demon Bot, and the terrifying God Bot. Earn leaderboard points for winning.

💣
Bomb Game

A high-stakes party game where players pass a ticking bomb. Duel mode, bot mode, or battle royale — last one standing wins.

🏆
Leaderboards

Persistent server rankings for fight wins and bomb game victories. Compete for the top spot and prove your dominance.

🌑
Quest System

Complete the Reaper Quest to unlock the legendary Reaper species. Progress is saved and carries across sessions.

⚡
Awakenings

Push your Cyborg to its limits — 25 wins, 500 damage, 15 ULT uses — and awaken into the transcendent Mechangel.

🔄
Class Switching

Not happy with your species? Switch every 3 hours. Balance strategy with luck — some rolls are more valuable than others.

💬
Persistent Channels

Set a dedicated duel channel for your server. All fight requests route there, keeping your server organised.

All Commands

/rollRoll for your species. Each roll costs a chance — roll wisely.Core
/speciesView your current species, stats, and abilities.Core
/species changeSwitch your species (3 hour cooldown).Core
/fight @userChallenge another player to a 1v1 RPG duel.Combat
/fight attackAttack during your turn in an active fight.Combat
/fight healHeal during your turn in an active fight.Combat
/fight ultUse your species' ultimate ability (when off cooldown).Combat
/botfightFight an AI opponent. Choose difficulty: easy, medium, hard, or impossible.Combat
/bomb startStart a bomb party game in the current channel.Games
'pass @userPass the bomb to another player during a bomb game.Games
/leaderboardView the server's top fighters and bomb game champions.Social
/profile @userView another player's species, stats, and win record.Social
/questView your Reaper Quest progress. Fight to unlock the Reaper species.Quests
/awakeningCheck your Cyborg awakening progress toward Mechangel.Quests
/togglerequestsToggle whether you can receive fight requests from others.Settings
/setchannelSet a persistent duel channel for your server (admin only).Admin
/patchnotesView the latest LOZ update notes and change history.Info
Progression

Quests & Awakenings

Two paths to unlock species that can't be rolled. Earn them through dedication.

🌑
The Reaper Quest

Prove yourself worthy of Death's mantle. Win fights, deal damage, and survive long enough to claim the Reaper species — one of the strongest in the game with an instant-kill ultimate.

⭐ Reward: Reaper Species (25–38 ATK, Instant Kill ULT)
⚡🤖
Cyborg Awakening

Already a Cyborg? Push further. Achieve 25 wins, deal 500 total damage, and use your ULT 15 times to transcend into Mechangel — the fusion of machine and angel.
